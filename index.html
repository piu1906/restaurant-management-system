Paneer Tikka Masala

Paneer tikka masala is an Indian dish of paneer tikka cheese served in a spiced gravy. It is a vegetarian alternative to chicken tikka masala.

Chana Masala

Chana masala (also chole masala, or chole) is a chickpea curry originating in the Indian subcontinent.

Mix Veg

A simple and healthy curry or sabzi recipe prepared mainly with a choice of vegetables and paneer cubes. It is an ideal North Indian cuisine curry recipe.

Dal Fry

Dal Fry is a delicious and popular Indian dish made with lentils (toor dal), onions, tomatoes, spices and herbs.
